About Dikksha Corporation

Leading the digital transformation revolution in laboratory instrumentation since 2003

Our Story

Founded in 2003 in the heart of Bengaluru, Dikksha Corporation began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to revolutionize laboratory instrumentation through precision, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality.

Under the visionary leadership of CEO Hardik D., we have grown from a small startup to a globally recognized manufacturer of precision laboratory instruments. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships, and an unwavering focus on customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve clients across East Asia, the Middle East, and beyond, providing cutting-edge analytical, biotechnology, and environmental testing equipment to pharmaceutical companies, research institutions, and educational organizations worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide precision laboratory instruments that enable scientific breakthroughs and advance research across pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and environmental sectors. We are committed to delivering innovative solutions that meet the highest standards of accuracy, reliability, and performance.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in laboratory instrumentation, recognized for our technological excellence, customer-centric approach, and contribution to scientific advancement. We envision a future where our instruments enable researchers to make groundbreaking discoveries that benefit humanity.

Our Values

Innovation, Quality, Integrity, and Customer Success form the foundation of everything we do. We believe in continuous improvement, ethical business practices, and building lasting relationships with our clients and partners.
